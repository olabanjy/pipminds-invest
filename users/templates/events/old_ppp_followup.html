What is this migration all about?
The migration process involves moving all our investors and partners account and investment records online for an improved user experience through “Pipminds Invest” - our new investment app

The migration of user accounts and investment records occurs in two stages;
Migration of sponsors accounts (PPP) and
Migration of Investment records (CIP & HIP).

The first phase of migration has been concluded. This will enable registered sponsors (Pipminds Partner) have access to their referral codes. With these referral codes, each partner can share to new investors joining the CIP or HIP investment programs through them.

The second phase of migration - migration of Investment records for users with active CIP and HIP investment is currently ongoing thus, existing partners and Investors can expect to see their their PPP earnings and investments records updated on their dashboard once this phase is done. Partners and investors would be notified via email when this phase is completed.

To this end, all partners are encouraged to direct their new CIP/HIP referrals to register and invest via the our new online portal - Pipminds Invest
