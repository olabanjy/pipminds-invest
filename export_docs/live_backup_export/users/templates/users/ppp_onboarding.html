Overview

This is a reward program for incredible marketers and brand promoters. We reward great performances with a percentage commission every time you tell anyone about PIPminds International and further make them subscribe to any of our products and services using your unique referral code.

Being a partner gives you access to the Corporate Investment Pool

The PIP Minds Partners Program sets aside about 5% of profit monthly to service the multi-level marketing program and rewards its partners up to the third level. This means that you get 2% at the 1st level, 1% at the 2nd level and 0.5% at the 3rd level monthly.

This reward program spans the duration of the investment and is payable every 30 days. Being a partner gives you access to the Corporate Investment Pool and also a dashboard where you can report referrals and track how much you have made on commission.

The work is made easier for you as you’re provided with a set of marketing tools customized for you to work with. Being a partner comes with leadership awards and recognition as well as travelling and other incentives.
